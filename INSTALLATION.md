## The Easy Way

No external services needed! This is a native Home Assistant integration.

### 1. Install via HACS
- **Home Assistant** ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Devices & Services ‚Üí **HACS** tab
- Click **"+ Explore & Download Repositories"**

### 2. Add Device in Home Assistant
- **Settings ‚Üí Devices & Services ‚Üí Create Integration**
- Search for **"Tempwise BLE"**
- Enter your device's Bluetooth MAC address and characteristic UUID

**That's it!** The sensor appears automatically in Home Assistant.
